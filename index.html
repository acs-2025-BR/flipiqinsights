FlipIQ Insights

FlipIQ Insights is a lightweight reporting tool that generates research summaries for shop owners based on their own Etsy sales data. The application does not alter or manage listings and is not a dashboard or marketplace replacement. It simply retrieves authorized shop data through the Etsy Open API and presents it in a private report format to help sellers better understand trends and performance.

Privacy Policy

FlipIQ Insights only accesses data from your Etsy shop after you grant explicit authorization through the official Etsy API. We do not store, sell, or share your data with third parties. Data is used only to generate reports for your own shop performance. No listing edits or customer data are modified by this application.
